**Design Movement:** Cold War Brutalism meets Silicon Valley Defense Tech
**Core Principles:**
1. Raw structural honesty — exposed grid lines, stark contrast, zero decorative fluff

**Typography System:** Display: Space Grotesk 800 (massive, architectural). Body: IBM Plex Mono 400 (technical, honest). Labels: IBM Plex Mono 600 uppercase.

## Selected Design: Stealth Tech (Option 3)

The Stealth Tech aesthetic is the strongest choice for ChaosTech Defense. It visually communicates cutting-edge capability without being aggressive or immature. The deep black + electric cyan palette evokes advanced avionics and modern defense systems — exactly the credibility signal needed for DoD and prime contractor audiences. The glass-panel cards and hexagonal motifs feel premium and purpose-built, not generic.
